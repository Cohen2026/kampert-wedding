Our story

We didn't see it coming.
But He did.

Our journey began unexpectedly during a season when both of us were focused on becoming better individually, growing in purpose, clarity, and direction. What started as a simple conversation quickly became something we both recognized as different. There was an ease in how we spoke, a depth that came naturally, and a peace that continued to confirm itself over time.

We come from two very different worlds. Cleo is Haitian, raised in Orlando, Florida. Paul is Dutch, from the Netherlands, living in Tampa. Different cultures, different backgrounds, different paths — yet somehow our lives crossed in Florida at exactly the right time, in a way neither of us could have planned or predicted.

From the very beginning, we chose to get to know each other intentionally, moving beyond surface-level conversations into purpose, family, values, and vision. We asked real questions, had honest and sometimes uncomfortable conversations, and built something greater than we could have imagined.

Through it all, we found a friendship and a relationship grounded in faith, intentionality, and purpose, with God guiding each step forward.

Today, we are grateful for the way our paths crossed and for everything that has led us to this moment. By God's grace, we look forward to stepping into this next chapter as Mr. & Mrs. Kampert.

It would be an honor to have you join us as our guest on our special day.

Good questions

In case you
were wondering.

  • The ceremony and cocktail hour are held outdoors, so plan accordingly. Dinner, toasts, cake, and the rest of the evening take place inside. Dress for a warm Florida night and we will take care of the rest.

  • Late July in Orlando is hot and humid. Expect daytime highs in the low-to-mid 90s°F with a heat index well above 100°F. Brief but heavy afternoon thunderstorms are nearly a daily occurrence and typically clear quickly. By evening temperatures settle into the mid-to-upper 80s°F. We recommend light, breathable fabrics under your formal attire and staying well hydrated. We will have hand fans and cold towels on standby.

  • This is an adults-only celebration. We kindly ask that children not attend. We truly appreciate your understanding and cannot wait to celebrate with you.

  • Your presence is everything. If you'd still like to celebrate with a gift, visit the Registry section of this website — our registry and QR code will be posted there before July 23rd.

  • The on-site lot is complimentary, with additional parking available in the back of the café. Street parking along Lee Road is also an option. Rideshare is welcomed and arguably wiser if you'd like a second glass of champagne.

  • Use the RSVP form above to reach us. It comes directly to the couple and is the fastest way to get a response. We look forward to hearing from you.
